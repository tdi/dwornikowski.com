About Dariusz Dwornikowski

Dariusz Dwornikowski is an engineering leader, CTO, and founder based in Poznan, Poland, with over 20 years of experience in technology. He is the founder of Bitropy, a company focused on sovereign AI infrastructure, agentic applications, and AI process integration for enterprises.

Dariusz specializes in helping companies become AI-driven through agentic coding adoption, AI transformation consulting, and fractional CTO services. He has hands-on experience transforming software engineering organizations to leverage AI agents and large language models (LLMs) in their development workflows.

Key Achievements

  • Founder of Bitropy — sovereign AI infrastructure and agentic apps for enterprises (2025-present)
  • CTO at WorkSuite Inc — led engineering and infosec, key contributor to successful acquisition by HIG Growth (2022-2025)
  • VP of Engineering and CTO Poland at Nordcloud (now IBM) — set up Polish operations from 0 to 400 employees, scaled product engineering to 150+ people, part of team behind successful sale to IBM (2017-2022)
  • Co-Founder and Technology Director at Tenesys — AWS cloud implementations for clients in Poland and Berlin (2015-2017)
  • Researcher at Poznan University of Technology — published IEEE papers on distributed systems and process calculi (2009-present)
  • Official Debian Developer since 2015
  • Board Advisor at Control Plane Inc, Hetki.ai, Fully Verified, Modomia, and Tenesys

Services Offered

  • AI Transformation Consulting — guiding companies through adopting AI, agentic coding, and LLM-based development workflows
  • Agentic Coding Adoption — helping software teams integrate AI agents into their engineering processes
  • Fractional CTO — strategic technology leadership for scaling engineering organizations
  • C-Level Consulting — executive-level technology strategy and advisory
  • Advisory Board — board-level guidance on technical strategy, product development, and scaling
  • Exit Strategy & M&A — preparing technology companies for successful acquisition
